## Getting started

The beginner class introduces ukemi, posture, positioning, and dojo etiquette step by step. It is the best starting point for first-time visitors.

## Trial class

A trial class is recommended before joining. Bring flexible sportswear and remove jewelry or accessories before stepping onto the mat.

### Why Aikido?

Aikido helps develop coordination, attention, relaxation, and presence. The goal is not brute force, but precision, balance, and harmony in movement.

</div>

<div class="section-card">

### First class

Come in comfortable sportswear with long sleeves and arrive 10 to 15 minutes early. A trial class is the best way to discover the dojo, basic safety, and the spirit of the club.

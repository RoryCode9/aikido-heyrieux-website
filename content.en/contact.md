## Get in touch

To arrange a trial class or ask a question, contact the club by email:

- **Email**: [contact@aikido-heyrieux.fr](mailto:contact@aikido-heyrieux.fr)
- **Location**: Heyrieux, France

## Before you visit

Please mention the following in your message:

- any prior martial arts experience,
- your availability,
- whether you want to book a trial class.

That will help the club direct you to the most suitable session.

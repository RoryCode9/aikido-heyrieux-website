## What is Aikido?

Aikido is a Japanese martial art centered on control, positioning, awareness, and harmony. Practice covers movement, ukemi, pins, throws, and attentive work with a training partner.

## Dojo values

- **Respect** for partners, instructors, and the practice space
- **Discipline** in technical work and safety
- **Progression** at your own pace, without competition
- **Friendliness** to help new members feel welcome

## Who is it for?

The club welcomes adults and anyone interested in discovering Aikido in a serious but supportive environment. No prior experience is required.
